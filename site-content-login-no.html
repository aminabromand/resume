Me, Myself & I

I am a confident, intelligent, creative man enjoying this amazing life. I am honest, reliable and straightforward. I am optimistic and usually smiling. I am tender. I will laugh with you, and I will cry with you. I have been told I am funny, unique and charming. When I was younger I suppose I was a bit arrogant and full of myself. I am older now and I think I know myself better and understand my responsibility in relation to others. Perhaps arrogance has become confidence.
